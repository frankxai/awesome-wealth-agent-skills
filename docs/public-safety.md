# Public Safety Rules

This repo is public-safe by design. It can describe how to build a wealth research operating system, but it must not expose private financial reality or imply regulated authority.

## Do Publish

- Public source links.
- GitHub metadata and review dates.
- Research categories.
- Mock examples.
- Risk labels.
- Non-advisory architecture.

## Do Not Publish

- Account names or balances.
- Wallet addresses tied to real holdings.
- Seed phrases, private keys, recovery shares, custody locations, or multisig signer identities.
- Live investment strategy, order instructions, or target allocations.
- Tax, legal, securities, accounting, or compliance conclusions.
- Promises, expected returns, or certainty language.

## Required Language

Use this boundary where relevant:

> This is research organization and system architecture, not financial, investment, legal, tax, accounting, or securities advice. Humans retain authority over capital and execution.

## Autonomy Boundary

Allowed in public:

- Research.
- Tool comparison.
- Strategy memo templates.
- Backtest and simulation language.
- Human approval gates.

Not allowed in public:

- Agent-held real wallets.
- Live trading keys.
- Autonomous capital movement.
- Recovery material.
- Private data routing details.
